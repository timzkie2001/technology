Benefits of using technology in Education.

Increased collaboration and communication

Technology promotes collaboration.During Covid-19,technology plays a vital role in online class session.It is an alternative way in teaching and learning because it's dangerous to conduct face to face classes.Despite of pandemic this scenario really increased collaboration and communication between teachers and students.For example,if there's a clarification that the students would like to ask he/she can message his/her teacher or classmates.When the Covid-19 was very rampant in our country it really difficult to adjust and learn properly.But,because of technology it really help us to move forward.

Learning Opportunities

We all know that not all information can we get in books.As a student,sometimes we need to become resourceful in finding an information to the internet using our gadgets.It is also a great opportunity because we can learn in our own.We can discover something and apply it in real life situation.In learning it is not limited but it depends to our determination.Through the use of technology it makes our work easier and finish it on time.

Prepares students for the future

Since technology permeates every aspect of life, it is crucial that it be incorporated into the existing learning environment for kids. The usage of technology in the classroom will increase students' familiarity with common software that they will use in their future employmentTeachers want to prepare their pupils, and using technology is a terrific method to achieve that. Students must be able to comprehend the fundamentals because it will have a significant impact on their life in the future. Their future personal and professional life will be heavily reliant on the use of computers and the internet.

Improved teacher productivity and efficiency

Teachers may use technology to boost productivity, apply practical digital tools to promote student learning opportunities, and boost engagement and support from students. Additionally, it gives teachers the ability to customise learning and enhance their teaching strategies. By lowering the cost of physical teaching materials, improving the effectiveness of educational programs, and making the most use of instructor time, technology can assist schools.

It offers students an enjoyable and interesting learning experience

Today's students must not only learn, but also enjoy themselves while doing so. Students have the chance to have fun while learning thanks to educational technology, which will also keep them motivated and enthusiastic about their studies.
